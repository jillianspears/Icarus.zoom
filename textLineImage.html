Motor Types

Oxidizer Tank

Holds the oxidizer required for combustion in hybrid engines.

Fuel Grain

Solid fuel core that burns with the oxidizer stream.

Igniter

Triggers combustion of the solid propellant.

Burn Surface

Shapes the combustion pattern and burn rate.

Bleeder Valve

Used to release pressure in the chamber post-test.

Pressure Chamber

Withstands high internal forces during engine firing.
